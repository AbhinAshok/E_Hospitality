Our Services

E-Hospitality provides a wide range of healthcare services to meet the needs of patients, doctors, and administrators.

  • Appointment Booking

    Schedule appointments with your healthcare providers easily and receive reminders to ensure timely care.

  • Online Medicine Purchase

    Buy prescribed medicines online and have them delivered directly to your doorstep for added convenience.

  • Health Tips and Resources

    Access health articles, tips, and resources to help you make informed decisions about your well-being.

  • Access to Medical Records

    View your medical history, lab results, and other important records securely anytime, anywhere.

  • Virtual Consultations

    Consult with doctors online for quick guidance on minor health issues or follow-ups without visiting the clinic.

  • Billing and Payment Gateway

    Manage your bills and make secure payments for services received through a convenient online payment system.

  • Health Monitoring Tools

    Track vital health metrics like blood pressure, blood sugar, and more through integrated monitoring tools.
